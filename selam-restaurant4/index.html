About Us

Selam Restaurant was founded in 2010 with a vision to bring the rich culinary traditions of Ethiopia to the heart of the city.

Our name, "Selam," means "peace" in Amharic, reflecting our commitment to providing a welcoming and serene dining environment. Our chefs are dedicated to preparing dishes that are not only delicious but also a true representation of Ethiopian culture and heritage. We believe in the power of good food to bring people together and create lasting memories.
